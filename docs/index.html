Frequently Asked Questions

What is Dwello?

Dwello is an iOS app that helps homeowners track maintenance, log repairs, manage contractors, and get smart reminders for upcoming tasks.

Is my data secure?

Yes. All data is stored in your private iCloud account via Apple CloudKit. We cannot access your data. It's encrypted in transit and at rest.

What's included in Free vs Premium?

Free includes 20 log entries/month, basic reminders, and 3-month history. Premium unlocks unlimited logging, AI predictions, full analytics, PDF reports, and multi-home support.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open Settings on your iPhone → tap your Apple ID at the top → Subscriptions → select Dwello → tap Cancel Subscription. Your access continues until the end of the current billing period.

How do I delete my account and data?

In the app: Profile → Settings → Delete Account. Or email us at keshav-krishnan@outlook.com with the subject "Delete Account." Processing takes up to 30 days.

Does it sync across devices?

Yes. Dwello uses iCloud to sync your data across all Apple devices signed into the same iCloud account.
