How to Cancel Your Subscription

Subscriptions are managed through Apple. To cancel, follow these steps on your iPhone or iPad:

1 Open Settings

Tap the Settings app on your device.

2 Tap Your Name

At the top of Settings, tap your name to access your Apple ID settings.

3 Go to Subscriptions

Tap Subscriptions to see all your active subscriptions.

4 Select HomeBase

Find and tap HomeBase in your subscription list.

5 Cancel Subscription

Tap Cancel Subscription and confirm. You'll retain access until the end of your current billing period.

Important: You must cancel at least 24 hours before your renewal date to avoid being charged for the next period. Deleting the app does not cancel your subscription.

How to Delete Your Account & Data

You have full control over your data. Here's how to remove it:

Delete Data Within the App

You can delete individual maintenance logs, photos, contractor records, and appliance entries directly from within the app. Navigate to the item and use the delete option.

Delete All App Data

To erase all HomeBase data at once, use the data management option in the app's settings. This removes all your content from iCloud.

Request Complete Deletion

If you'd like us to ensure all data associated with your account is fully removed, send a request to keshav-krishnan@outlook.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request." We'll process it within 30 days.

Remember: Deleting the app doesn't automatically cancel your subscription. Make sure to cancel through your Apple ID settings first to avoid future charges.

Report a Bug

Found something that doesn't work quite right? We want to know about it. When reporting a bug, include as much detail as possible so we can track it down quickly:

  • What happened -- describe the issue clearly
  • What you expected -- tell us the behavior you anticipated
  • Steps to reproduce -- how can we recreate the problem?
  • Device & iOS version -- e.g., iPhone 15 Pro, iOS 19.3
  • Screenshots -- attach them if possible

Send bug reports to keshav-krishnan@outlook.com with "Bug Report" in the subject line. We review every report and prioritize fixes.
